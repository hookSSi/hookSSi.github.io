## Logistic Sigmoid Function

$$
\sigma(x) = {1 \over 1 + e^{-x}}
$$

![sigmoid](https://mlnotebook.github.io/img/transferFunctions/sigmoid.png)

## Hyperbolic Tangent Function

$$
tanh(x) = { {e^x - e^{-x}} \over {e^x + e^{-x}} }
$$
